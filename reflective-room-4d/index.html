How it works

Don't read. Explore.

Move through the room. Hover over the objects — the plants, the table, the signs above the doors. Each one reacts, revealing something.

Not everything shows itself at once.

When you find a room that calls to you, walk in. Inside each room, open the notebook and write whatever comes up. There is no right way to respond.

Your words stay on your device until you choose to clear them. Sound is part of the experience.

○ Your writing stays with you. Nothing is uploaded or shared.
